Achievments


1. Awarded Formula Student Award  worth 3000 GBP by Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE) out of 52 teams internationally
2. First Indian team  to implement an Aluminium Honeycomb Monocoque chassis for weight reduction and greatly improved rigidity
3. High power Brushless DC Motors with Regenerative Braking were implemented to achieve 0-60 kmph in 2.18 sec   4.Carbon Fibre Underbody Diffuser developed using Full Car CFD simulations to provide more than 10 Kgs of downforce at the average speed for improved traction 
5. Hybrid Planetary gearbox developed to achieve a higher gear ratio with 40% reduction in weight and smaller packaging 
6. Custom Battery Management System (BMS) developed in-house to provide voltage monitoring, logging and temperature measurement for a 403.2 V, 6.66 kWh battery pack
